Frequently Asked Questions


1. Who can apply for nomination and entity formation?

Ans. Any Faculty/Student who enrolled in the Chandigarh university can apply for any entity.

2. Is propIosal necessary with entity formation ?

Ans. Yes, Proposal is a necessary document to form any entity.

3. How many entities can be formed?

Ans. One individual person can form any one entity.

4. Can a student make an entity ?

Ans. Yes, any student can form an entity.

5. What is the process to nominate myself as secretary and joint secretary?

Ans.Students/Faculty who don’t want to create new entity they can follow the below steps:
1> Check the existing Clubs provided by the university document name: List of Club@cu.
2> After choosing the club name, students/faculty have to fill the nomination form for any position[Faculty Advisor/Faculty Co-Advisor/ Secretary/Joint Secretary].
3> After Filling the form faculty/.student can submit the form by google form.

6. What type of activities/events can be done under entities?

Ans. Refer, the Guideline Document.
